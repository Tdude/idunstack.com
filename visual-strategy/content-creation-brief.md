## Brand Photography Style Guide

### Lighting Guidelines
- **Natural light preferred**: Large windows, soft shadows
- **Golden hour**: Warm, optimistic mood
- **Avoid harsh flash**: Soft, diffused artificial light only
- **Consistent color temperature**: Daylight balanced (5600K)

### Composition Rules
- **Rule of thirds**: Dynamic but balanced compositions
- **Leading lines**: Guide eye to important elements
- **Negative space**: Breathing room, Scandinavian minimalism
- **Depth of field**: Selective focus for hierarchy

### Color Treatment
- **Natural color grading**: Subtle, realistic enhancement
- **Brand color integration**: Clothes, props, environment
- **Avoid oversaturation**: Clean, professional appearance
- **Consistent white balance**: Accurate color representation

### Props and Styling
- **Quality materials**: Good furniture, professional tools
- **Swedish design**: IKEA-adjacent but higher quality
- **Plants and nature**: Living elements, not artificial
- **Technology**: Latest devices, clean setups
- **Coffee culture**: Natural Swedish workplace elements
